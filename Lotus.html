100% natural Lotus essential oil of the highest quality.

Botanical name: medicinal lotus (lotus officinalis), large-flowered magnolia (Magnolia grandiflora)

Family: (lotusliaceae)

Method of production: Two types of essential oil are obtained from magnolia flowers or leaves by steam distillation. Neroli flowers are extracted with petroleum ether. A greenish-yellow concrete with a melting point of 600 is obtained in a yield of 1.2-1.6%. Steam distillation of concrete is obtained in a yield of 10% as a semi-solid light yellow oil with a characteristic odor of flowers. From the leaves of magnolia by steam distillation with a yield of 0.1-0.15% get a greenish oil with a very pleasant smell. The oil becomes viscous when exposed to air. Plant part used: flowers, leaves

Aroma: light, fresh, creamy, uplifting

Color: light yellow
