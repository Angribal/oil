HYDROLATES, FLOWER WATERS

Hydrolat - is an aqueous solution containing water-soluble molecules of plant materials. Hydrolates are obtained during the production of essential oils by steam distillation of plants at a temperature of 100 ° C, using water that does not contain any impurities. In this case, water turns into steam, followed by condensation of the steam into a liquid (distillate). As a result, such water absorbs most of the biologically active substances from the plant and, in addition, also trace amounts of essential oils that did not separate during precipitation.
